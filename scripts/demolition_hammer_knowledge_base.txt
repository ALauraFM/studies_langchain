Enhanced Knowledge Base for the XP500 Demolition Hammer
XP500 Demolition Hammer

Specifications:

Motor: High-power electric motor (1500 W).

Impacts per minute: 0–4000 ipm.

Impact energy: 20 J.

Weight: 5 kg.

Connectivity: Wi-Fi (for monitoring and diagnostics).

Troubleshooting

Does not turn on:

Check that the battery is fully charged and properly connected.
Wait 30 seconds after connecting the battery to allow the system to initialize.
Make sure the on/off switch is in the correct position.
If the problem persists, contact technical support at 0800 555 5555.

Does not reverse:

Check that the reverse lever is properly engaged.
Check for any obstruction preventing the bit from moving.
If the problem persists, turn off the demolition hammer and contact technical support.
Wi-Fi does not connect:
Check that the demolition hammer’s Wi-Fi is enabled.
Make sure you are entering the correct Wi-Fi network password.
Restart the demolition hammer and try connecting again.
If the problem persists, consult the manual for connectivity troubleshooting instructions.

Overheating:

Turn off the demolition hammer and allow it to cool down for a few minutes.
Check that the air vents are not obstructed.
Avoid continuous use for long periods; take regular breaks.

Excessive vibration:

Check that the bit is properly secured.

Make sure the working surface is level and stable.

If the vibration persists, turn off the demolition hammer and contact technical support.

Simplified User Manual
Battery charging:

Connect the charger to the demolition hammer and to a power outlet.

Wait until the charging indicator light turns green.

Bit installation:

Insert the bit into the chuck and tighten it firmly using the wrench.

Operation:

Hold the demolition hammer firmly with both hands.

Turn on the tool and position the bit on the work surface.

Apply gradual pressure to start drilling.

Use the reverse lever to change the rotation direction of the bit.

Take regular breaks to prevent overheating.

Maintenance:

Clean the demolition hammer after each use.

Periodically check the condition of the bit and replace it if necessary.

Store the demolition hammer in a dry and safe place.

Notes

Always use personal protective equipment (PPE) when operating the demolition hammer.

Consult the full manual for detailed information on the use and maintenance of the XP500 demolition hammer.

If you have any questions or problems, contact technical support at 0800 555 5555.
